What is Glee

Glee is about a high school show choir at the fictional McKinley High School in Ohio run by a Spanish teacher, Will Shuester. The general plot of each episode typically has to do with the club preparing for competitions as well as the students' personal lives. The show also features several covers of songs in each episode that typically move the plot or lesson of the show along.

Glee was also known for tackling serious issues including gender, pregnancy, homophobia, depression, and disabilites. It is famous for being one of the early representations of LGBTQ+ young adults on television.

Reception

The show aired from May 2009 to March 2015, had 121 episodes, and six seasons. It was fairly well received by audiances having been nominated for 19 Emmys and winning 2 Golden Globes for best Comedy.

Characters

(https://akns-images.eonline.com/)

Glee featured a large ensemble of characters and is known for their diverse cast. They include: Will Shuester, the original members of the Glee club, guidance counselor Emma, menacing cheerleading coach Sue Sylvester as well as other characters in the school as well as their families.

An important aspect of the show are the members of the club. Some of the central characters that the show focuses on are:
    Rachel Berry- The co-captain of the show who is seen as an over-achieving yet talented drama queen.
    Finn Hudson - The quarterback of the football team who is forced by Will to join the club, later becoming the captain
    Kurt Hummel - a talented member of the team with similar goals of Broadway to Rachel who comes to terms with his sexuality
    Mercedes Jones - a powerful singer who hopes to become a famous vocalist
    Quinn Fabray - a sterotypical mean girl cheerleader who later joins Glee after becoming pregnant
    Tina Cohen-Chang - a shy member of the group who expresses herself through her clothing
    Santana Lopez - a seemingly cruel cheerleader who discovers that she is a lesbian
    Mike Chang - another football player who is torn between his love for dance and pleasing his father
    Brittany S. Pierce - a ditsy blonde cheerleader who is a talented dancer and discovers that she is a math prodigy
