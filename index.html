Glee

This project uses a series of sources from academic research and editorials in order to analyze the representation of underrepresentated groups in the television show Glee.
I am not and do not consider myself an expert on any of these topics and will be using research from experts and academics in these areas to present my findings and will not make conclusions on my own.

Trigger Warning: The topics discussed include mistreatment of underepresented groups and some clips that are included may include offensive language.
